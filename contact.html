Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to messages?

I typically respond to all messages within 24 hours. WhatsApp messages usually get the fastest response, often within a few hours during business days.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, I offer free 15-minute discovery calls to discuss your project requirements and see if we're a good fit. For more detailed consultations, I offer paid sessions starting at $99/hour.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A standard Shopify store setup takes 3-5 days, while custom AI automation projects can take 2-4 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients from all over the world. My primary focus is helping entrepreneurs in South Asia, but I welcome clients from any location. All communication can be conducted in English.
